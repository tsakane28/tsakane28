## About

I'm a self-employed full-stack developer building software that solves real problems for real people — one project at a time.

My work follows a simple pattern: spot a friction point, build something that removes it, and ship it properly. Not MVPs that gather dust. Production systems that people actually use.

<br/>

**Currently building — FinTech & Financial Intelligence**

**[ZimFlow Forge](https://github.com/tsakane28/zimflow-forge)** — An FX operations and intelligence platform that automates RBZ exchange rate data extraction, monitors 40+ currency pairs, and transforms government documents into real-time dashboards and actionable financial intelligence.

<br/>

**Previously shipped**

- 🎟️ **[RSRVD Tickets](https://github.com/tsakane28/rsrvd-tickets)** — High-availability event ticketing with USSD offline access, EcoCash/Paynow payments, and serverless QR delivery.
- 📄 **[DoneIn5](https://github.com/tsakane28/donein5)** — Professional PDF invoices in under 60 seconds, entirely client-side. No account. No backend. No friction.
- 🗜️ **[Browser File Compressor](https://github.com/tsakane28/file-compressor)** — Up to 95% compression, zero uploads. Every byte stays on your device.

<br/>

**Background**

Two years at the **Zimbabwe National Geospatial and Space Agency (ZINGSA)** gave me enterprise architecture and ERP customization expertise. I care about readable code, systems that don't fall over, and interfaces that work.
